The information you give will be emailed to our customer resolution team. Your data will be deleted once the case is resolved.

When to use this pattern

Enquiry forms should always be available to a user.

If a user submits an application, include a link to the enquiry form in follow-up communications.

Guidance

It can be used:

  • with a variety of enquiry and support types
  • with optional contact details

It can include:

  • information on how the service will store and use the information users give
  • a list of options for the subject of the enquiry
